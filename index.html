Welcome to Broadway for Everyone!

Broadway shapes the type of theatre we both know and love today. Our website was made specifically to help people explore the world of theatre; we do this by providing information on current shows, meanwhile diving into the history of theater, and showcasing what makes Broadway so special.

At Broadway for Everyone, we offer a guide crafted to better the audiences experience. This guide will share ideas and helpful tips to make sure Broadway will be, and stays, accessible and fun for everyone. Knowing what to expect can make seeing a performance even more memorable, and our website helps make it easy to get the most out of every visit.
